EASIEST Method 1: Right-Click on Image

1 Go to your ImgBB page (e.g., https://ibb.co/VpqzSG93)
2 RIGHT-CLICK directly on the image (not the page background)
3 Select:
  • Chrome/Edge: "Copy image address"
  • Firefox: "Copy Image Location"
  • Safari: "Copy Image Address"
4 Paste in admin panel ‚Üí Done! ‚úÖ

Method 2: From Embed Codes Section

1 On the ImgBB page, scroll down below the image
2 Look for "Embed codes" section
3 Find "Direct link" (first option usually)
4 Click the copy button or select the text and copy
5 Paste in admin panel ‚Üí Done! ‚úÖ

RECOMMENDED Method 3: Use Admin Upload (No URLs Needed!)

1 In admin panel, click "Upload Image" tab
2 Drag & drop your image OR click to browse
3 Image uploads automatically to ImgBB
4 Direct URL is filled automatically ‚Üí Save! ‚úÖ
